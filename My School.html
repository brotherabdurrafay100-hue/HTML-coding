Sports

Sport is any physical activity or game that involves skill, competition, and physical effort. It can be played individually or in teams, with the aim of improving fitness, enjoyment, or winning against opponents. Sports test strength, speed, strategy, and teamwork while also promoting discipline and fair play. They are a source of entertainment and culture worldwide, from traditional games to modern global events like the Olympics. Sports help maintain physical health, reduce stress, and build social connections. They can be recreational or professional, uniting people across nations, languages, and cultures through shared passion and enthusiasm for performance and competition.
